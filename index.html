URL shortener service

This service will create a shortend version of a long URL


Usage:

http://127.0.0.1:4000/new/http://www.duckduckgo.com generates:

{"original_url":"http://www.duckduckgo.com","short_url":"http://127.0.0.1:4000/new/c40e"}

If you go to http://127.0.0.1:4000/new/c40e you will be redirected to http://www.duckduckgo.com
